Spheroscopic Privacy Policy

General

Spheroscopic only logs anonymized analytics to bring us ability of debuging the app. The bug report only sends device os, app version, user's time zone, app's screen resolution, and some information about app. And no personal data is included.

Changes to This Policy

You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes. By using the Spheroscopic app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
